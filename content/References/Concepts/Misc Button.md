**Pin Terminal Introduction**
- "+": positive pole of the led
- " - ": negative pole of the led
- " NC ": Normal Close
- " NO ": Normal Open
- " C " : Common
- Connect " NO " and " C " : The circuit is alway open unless press the button.
- Connect " NC " and " C " : The circuit is alway close unless press the button.
