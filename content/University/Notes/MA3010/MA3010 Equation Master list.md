# Humidity of Air
*Use celcius instead of kelvin here*
## Specific and relative humidity
$$ \omega = \frac{m_v}{m_a} \quad \quad \phi = \frac{m_v}{m_g}=\frac{P_v}{P_g} \quad \text{where} \quad P_g = P_{\text{sat} @ T} $$
Given in exam:

$$ \phi = \frac{\omega P}{(0.622 + \omega) P_g} \quad \omega = \frac{0.622 \phi P_g}{P - \phi P_g}$$
## Specific enthalpy of Atmospheric Air
$$
h = h_a +\omega h_g \quad (kJ/kg\text{ dry air})
$$
## Dew Point Temperature
$$
T_{dp}=T_{sat@P_v}
$$
## Adiabatic Saturation Temperature / Wet Bulb Temperature
Given in exam:
$$
\omega_1 = \frac{c_p (T_2 - T_1) + \omega_2 h_{fg2}}{h_{g1} - h_{f2}}
$$
